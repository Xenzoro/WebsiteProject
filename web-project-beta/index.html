About Me

I am Jacob Martinez , a junior at UNLV pursuing a degree in Information Systems with a strong passion for coding and software development. My academic work and projects have given me hands on experience in C++, Java, web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with tools such as GitHub, Clion, IntelliJ, WebStorm, and Linux. Outside of school, I enjoy exploring technology by building computers, creating custom keyboards, and modifying controllers, which I have turned into a small side hustle. I also own and run a sticker shop with my girlfriend. In my free time I like playing games, spending time with friends, and working part time to support myself while continuing to grow my technical and problem solving skills.

(Joe mama)
